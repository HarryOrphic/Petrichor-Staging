A Little Bit About Us

We are an independent Coffee House and Bar based in Aintree Village, serving the freshest Coffee using our own blend, roasted by CC Roasters (formerly Crosby Coffee). The bar which operates from 12PM to close has a wide selection of Spirits, a wine list, 4 draught taps and a very varied selection of bottled and canned craft beers from the UK and across the globe!
We’re dog friendly as long as your dog’s friendly, so swing by with your furry friends on your next walk! A relaxing daytime atmosphere to bring the family to, or to sit later on with a quiet drink and watch the world go by. For more information on what we offer, head over to our Instagram page to see what events we have on and who we have playing on Thursday nights and Sunday Afternoons.
